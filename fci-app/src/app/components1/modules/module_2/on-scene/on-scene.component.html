Diesel exhaust

  • Classified by multiple organizations as “potential” or “likely” carcinogenic
  • Classified by International Agency for Research on Cancer as carcinogenic
  • Exposure occurs when trucks run in closed bays without proper ventilation
  • Can contaminate gear, ice machines, and other items stored in the bay area

PPE Off-gassing

  Module 2

PPE collects contaminants during fire

Contaminants continue off-gassing from PPE even after a fire, extending inhaled exposure:

  • Doffing of gear
  • Rehab, if near PPE
  • Exposure occurs when trucks run in closed bays without proper ventilation
  • Transporting gear in engine cab

Higher temperatures can accelerate off-gassing
